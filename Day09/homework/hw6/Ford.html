Ford became known worldwide for introducing the moving assembly line, which made car production much faster and more affordable. One of its most successful models, the Ford Model T, changed the automobile industry by making cars available to ordinary people. Over the years, Ford has produced many popular vehicles, such as the Mustang, F-150, and Explorer. The company also owns the Lincoln brand, which focuses on luxury cars. Ford has factories and offices in many countries and sells millions of vehicles around the world. Today, Ford continues to innovate by developing electric and hybrid vehicles to support a cleaner environment. It remains one of the most respected and influential car companies in the history of the automobile industry.
