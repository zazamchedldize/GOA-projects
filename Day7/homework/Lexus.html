Lexus

Lexus is a luxury car brand from Japan, owned by Toyota Motor Corporation. It was founded in 1989, and its main headquarters are in Nagoya, Japan. Lexus is known for producing high-quality, comfortable, and reliable luxury vehicles. Some of the most popular Lexus models are the RX, ES, and LX. The brand focuses on advanced technology, safety, and smooth driving experience, combining performance with elegance. Today, Lexus is one of the most recognized luxury car brands in the world, selling vehicles in many countries and continuing to innovate with hybrid and electric models.
