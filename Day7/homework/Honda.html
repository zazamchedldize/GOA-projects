Honda

Honda Motor Company is a famous Japanese car and motorcycle manufacturer. It was founded in 1948 by Soichiro Honda and Takeo Fujisawa. The company’s main office is in Tokyo, Japan. Honda first became popular for making motorcycles and later started producing cars, engines, and other power products. Some of Honda’s most famous car models are the Civic, Accord, and CR‑V, while in motorcycles, the CB series and Gold Wing are very popular. Honda is also known for innovation in engines, mobility, and technology. Today, Honda continues to make cars, motorcycles, electric vehicles, and robots, and it is one of the most respected automobile companies in the world.
