Lion

The lion is known as the king of the jungle. It is strong, fast, and lives in groups called prides.

Elephant

Elephants are the largest land animals. They are intelligent and have very strong family bonds.

Eagle

Eagles are powerful birds with excellent eyesight. They can spot prey from very far away.

Dolphin

Dolphins are friendly, intelligent marine animals. They communicate using clicks and whistles.

Wolf

Wolves live in packs and are known for their teamwork and communication skills.

Interesting Animal Facts

Some animals can communicate using sounds humans cannot hear. Others use colors or movement to send messages.
